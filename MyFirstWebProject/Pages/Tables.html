3 parts structure of the assignment

    Proposal 15% 1500 words. Submit within week 6. Talk about what you intend to do. What analysis you are going to use and a summary of what to do sources of information for the company are from their website and the boss of the company Structure:

  • Title: company’s name and brief description phrase as a reference for the project Client’s Requirement; Brief statement about the company, issues or opportunities and scope of the project.
  • Overall aim: what will be the project achieve- the mission of the project
    • Objectives; A list of detailed objectives that are active statements of what the project will do.
  • Parameters: Outline the scope of the project, and what will be included in the final output. Expansion of the scope of the project
  • Outcomes: what will the business be able after the project is done, an example is as a result of this consulting exercise, the client will be able to develop an understanding of the market sectors that are most attractive for the new business development Our approach; describe how you as a consultant will address the issue: such as what models to use. Eg. In this report, I will be using pestle analysis
  • Time plan: Identify important milestones and when the project should be concluded. Include a Gantt chart
  • Key personnel: The people involved and their roles in the project. No need to mention people. Just mention yourself as the consultant as the key personnel. Put my profile from my CV in this part. A Master of Science (MSc) degree holder in International Human Resources Management from Coventry University, the United Kingdom, with over 14 years of experience in Recruitment, Human Resources, Business Development, Data Analysis, Administration and IT. Hardworking, reliable, highly committed, disciplined individual and passionate job seeker with strong organizational skills and eager to learn. Articulate and highly receptive to feedback and can work well with or without supervision while striving to achieve high-quality results in tasks undertaken by, planning and prioritizing workload to meet tight deadlines and assist colleagues as teamwork is vital in a successful organization. Expertise in analyzing, articulating, and solving various problems.
  • Risk register: You must Identify any risks that may stop you from reaching any of your objectives or indeed the aim of the project. Impact and livelihood
  • Costing: How much will it cost the consultant/Consultant’s fees, expenses etc. Research how much consultants charge and include it. Break down every expense. Can be on a table

The CV must be included in the appendix
